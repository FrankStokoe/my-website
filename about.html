SELECTED WORKS ADAM TODHUNTER FUTURE ARCHIVE

Adam Todhunter is a multidisciplinary visual artist based in London, UK.

In pursuit of new ways of seeing. Adam’s practice is experimental, explorative and engaged in future visual technologies.

His work explores the unprecedented; an artist in dialogue with emerging visual technology. In this search for the new, Adam takes a hands-on, craft approach, celebrating incidental qualities; the unexpected.

Adam’s work is volumetric; not bound to the convention of traditional photographic capture. Instead capturing and creating in four dimensions (x,y,z, time). Exploring the untapped potential of non-human photographic lens’s such as 3D scanners, and stereo cameras.

World ALTERING not world BUILDING.

Adam’s work always maintains a connection to the real. Interested in how we can abstract and reframe captured reality as opposed to constructing new realities. Exploring the body and place; in the world we occupy both physically and digitally.
