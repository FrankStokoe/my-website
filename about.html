SELECTED WORKS ADAM TODHUNTER FUTURE ARCHIVE

Adam Todhunter is a multidisciplinary visual artist based in London, UK.

Adam’s practice is experimental, explorative and embracing emerging visual technologies in pursuit of future optics.

His work explores the unprecedented; an artist in dialogue with technology. Adam's hands-on approach to digital image-making, celebrates the incidental and found aesthetics of our contemporary visual world. Crafting the unimagined.

Adam’s work is volumetric; not bound to the conventions of traditional photography. Investigating the capabilities of capturing and creating in four dimensions (x,y,z, time). Exploring the untapped potential of non-human photographic lenses such as 3D scanners, and stereo cameras.

World ALTERING not world BUILDING.

Adam’s work always maintains a connection to the real. Interested in how we can abstract and reframe captured reality, as opposed to constructing new realities. Exploring the body and place as subjects; in the world we occupy, both physically and digitally.

From concept development through to execution, the studio collaborates with select clients and individuals to create bespoke visual languages.
